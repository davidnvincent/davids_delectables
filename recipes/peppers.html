    • Roasted Peppers

    Chef's Notes

    I love the color of these peppers. It's a wonderful decoration for any antipasto plate. I like to encircle the tray with the peppers. Then I put a stick of a good firm cheese and a tasty italian salami on top.

    Ingredients

    • 6 red or yellow peppers
    • Balsamic vinegar for drizzling olive oil for drizzling
    • 1 large garlic clove
    • Crushed firm cheese, cut into strips
    • Italian salami, sliced wafer thin

    Directions

    1. Place peppers on a cookie sheet.
    2. Roast the red peppers under the broiler until charred all over, about 10 minutes. Make sure you turn them at least once.
    3. Place in bowl and cover with plastic wrap for 15 minutes.
    4. Remove charred skins, remove seeds and stem, cut lengthwise into quarters.
    5. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar, olive oil and fresh garlic.
    6. Place in jar and refrigerate.
    7. Marinate for at least 1 day.
    8. Roll a slice of salami around each cheese stick.
    9. Place upon each segment of the peppers.
