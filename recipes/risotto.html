    • Risotto

    Chef's Notes

    This is my basic risotto recipe. It's delicious with fish, either on the side or put your shellfish or fish right in it and use it as a main course. You never get a fishy taste in this recipe. If you really want to go crazy, add some Sambuca to give it a kick. Total cooking time is approximately 30-40 minutes.

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup Arborio rice
    • 3T butter
    • 1T olive oil
    • 1/2 onion, chopped
    • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
    • 1 large garlic clove, minced
    • 2.5 cups fish, clam or chicken stock
    • 3T parmesan cheese
    • Dashes of cinnamon, nutmeg, clove, and allspice

    Directions

    1. Cook rice and 2 T butter in a heavy pot until golden and the butter is absorbed, about 10 minutes.
    2. Add olive oil, onion, parsley and garlic. Saut√© until onion is softened, about 3 minutes.
    3. Add stock, little by little, about a 1/4 cup at a time, stirring constantly.
    4. Cook slowly until stock is absorbed. Continue to add stock, 1/4 cup at a time until all the stock is used.
    5. When rice is done (but not mushy), add your spices, 1T butter, and the parmesan cheese.
